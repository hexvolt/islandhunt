What is IslandsHunt?

Adventure through the islands

IslandsHunt is a self-guided scavenger hunt for small teams of 2–4 people. You unlock missions on the go — each one rooted in a real experience, a local detail, or an unexpected moment somewhere on the islands. This is an adventure you and your teammates will remember.

What to expect

Missions worth showing up for

Every mission takes you somewhere specific and asks you to engage with it — really engage. You'll cross parts of the islands most people skip, see the skyline from angles you didn't know existed, and pick up pieces of local knowledge along the way. Quiet paths, changing views, and small discoveries turn a familiar island visit into a day you'll actually remember. By the time you're done, you'll know the islands from an angle most people never do.

01

Experience-based missions

Every mission is built around something real — an experience, a place, a view or a local detail. You're not just finding things; you're doing things.

02

Unlock as you go

Missions reveal themselves one at a time, keeping the day surprising. Submit text, a photo or video of you doing it to complete the mission.

03

Earn points, win prizes

Every completed mission scores your team points. We review your submissions and may reward you with extra points for creativity. Top teams are eligible for prizes from our local partners.

Before you go

Show up ready

What to bring

Pack light, but come ready

  • ✓A fully charged phone for the app, photos, and videos.
  • ✓Your teammates — you'll need them.
  • ✓A small phone tripod — missions include hands-free video and wide shots.
What to wear

Dress like you mean it

It's not a workout, you'll be just walking and biking. So dress for a nice day out. IslandsHunt creates a highlights video from your team's photos and video submissions, so choose something you'll be happy to see yourself in.
